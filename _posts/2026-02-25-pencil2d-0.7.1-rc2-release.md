> TL;DR: We need your help testing the upcoming Pencil2D v0.7.1 release! Download the latest release candidate from the links below and report any issues you find.

## A New Release Is Around the Corner!

Following the release of Pencil2D v0.7.0 back in October 2024, the team has been hard at work squashing bugs and polishing the experience. We're now getting close to releasing v0.7.1 and we'd love for as many users as possible to give the latest release candidate a try!

This release focuses on stability and quality-of-life improvements, packing a solid set of bug fixes and enhancements across two release candidates. The more people we get to test, the more confident we can be that the final release will provide the best experience possible.

## What's Changed

For the full list of changes since v0.7.0, see the [changelog on GitHub](https://github.com/pencil2d/pencil/compare/v0.7.0...v0.7.1-rc2).

## Feedback

Thank you for taking the time to read this, and a big thank you to everyone who help test this release candidate. Pencil2D is created and maintained by a very small group of volunteers and it would be impossible for us to thoroughly test it without the help of our community.

If you have any questions or encounter any issues, there are multiple channels you can reach us through. Either our [Discord server][discord] or our [forums][forums] are good places to bring up any questions and share feedback from testing.
